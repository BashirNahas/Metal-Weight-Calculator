Privacy Policy

Nahas MetalHub — developed by Synaptix

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no registration, and no tracking.

2. Data stored on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server:

  • Calculation history (metal type, shape, dimensions, result)
  • Favorite calculations
  • App preferences (language, theme, measurement unit)

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings.

3. Live metal price data

The app fetches current metal market prices from a third-party API (metals.dev). These requests contain no personal or identifying information — only a request for commodity price data. Please refer to the metals.dev privacy policy for details on their data handling.

4. Internet permission

The app requires internet access solely to retrieve live market prices. No other network activity occurs.

5. Third-party SDKs

The app uses no advertising SDKs, analytics libraries, or crash-reporting services. No data is shared with third parties.

6. Children's privacy

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age.

7. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
